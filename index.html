Zekang Cheng

I am from Linquan, Anhui, P.R. China. In 2010, I was enrolled in the Special Class of Gifted Young (SCGY) , and then I spent the next four years in University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) . I studied mechanics in USTC and got a bachelor degree in 2014. From 2014 to 2019, I did my Ph.D. in computational fluid dynamics at Cambridge University Engineering Department (CUED) supervised by Dr. Jie Li.
